Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Shaw
2023-04-20
travel

This is my first time in Kuala Lumpur, and I still have a lot of regrets when I say goodbye to my hometown.

My new team and I happily went to Port Klang, where we saw the most beautiful sunset, and I would like to thank Jiunn Jye for driving us to many places we didn’t know, including the sunset here.

I hope I can live well here. Some time ago, in my dream, I remembered those dark times, and when I woke up, I found myself in tears. I’m afraid to look back or even bring up that dark time, that helpless time when I was 16 years old.
