About Painting

Shaw
2023-05-08

Every time I draw a picture, I will think of the short month I spent in the extra-curricular class at the age of 12. I chatted happily with another friend every day, and there will be no more friends on the way home. Loneliness, that was a rare happiness in my youth, since then I have lost the light, so I sometimes draw when I am unhappy, it reminds me of the feeling of light shining on my body, although my painting is very bad.

Unfortunately, so far, no matter how I paint, I can’t find the feeling of light shining on my body. I lost the light, I lost myself, so I can’t paint with color.

I like the game GRIS very much. It is about how to achieve self-salvation. It has many colors, and each different color represents a different state of mind. In the end, GRIS found itself, and I don’t know what to do.

And latest two of pictures at my home in ChongQing.

Finally, I finish the GRIS and the ‘finish’ just for my paint scratte.
